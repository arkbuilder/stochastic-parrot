DLC

🔐 Cipher Seas

"Navigate encrypted waters and defend against digital pirates!"

Topic: Cybersecurity · 2 Islands · 6 Concepts · Tiers 1-2

Prerequisite: Complete the base AI/ML campaign

The Story

The seas aren't just full of monsters — they're full of digital threats. Captain Nemo discovers encrypted waters where data pirates steal information and cursed chests hide malware. Mastering cybersecurity concepts is the only way to navigate safely.

2 New Islands

🔒 Cipher Cove

Easy

Encounter: 🌫️ Cursed Fog

Encryption Phishing Firewall

Metaphors: Cipher Lockbox · Bait Barrel · Sea Wall

What You'll Learn
  • Encryption — Scrambling data so only the right key decodes it
  • Phishing — Deceptive attempts to steal information by impersonation
  • Firewall — A security barrier that monitors and controls traffic

🏰 Fortress Harbor

Medium

Encounter: 💥 Rival Battle

Authentication Malware Zero Trust

Metaphors: Gate Checkpoint · Cursed Chest · Watchtower

What You'll Learn
  • Authentication — Verifying that someone is who they claim to be
  • Malware — Malicious software designed to damage or exploit systems
  • Zero Trust — Never trust, always verify — every request is checked
