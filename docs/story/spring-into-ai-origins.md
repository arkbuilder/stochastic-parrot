# Spring into AI Origins — Story Context

Stochastic Parrot (Dead Reckoning: Memory Sea) draws direct inspiration from Week 3 of the **Spring into AI** challenge, where the theme was interactive tutorials that teach by doing.

Two standout entries shaped the project direction:

- **LinkLevel (Kyle Sebestyen)** — article-to-adventure loop with challenge progression and boss rounds.
  - Eric’s feature: https://ericrhea.com/spring-into-ai/week-3-link-level.html
- **Memory Palace (Myles Strait)** — Method of Loci gameplay for retention and recall.
  - Eric’s feature: https://ericrhea.com/spring-into-ai/week-3-memory-palace.html

## Why this mattered
These projects proved that AI education works better when the learner is inside a game loop:
- action over passive reading
- repetition over one-shot exposure
- measurable progress over vibes

Stochastic Parrot extends that philosophy into a navigable, story-first experience where memory mechanics, encounters, and progression all support concept retention.
