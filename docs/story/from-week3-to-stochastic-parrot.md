# From Week 3 to Stochastic Parrot

## The throughline
Week 3 asked a great question: *can tutorials become interactive systems that teach through play?*

- LinkLevel showed how content can become a quest.
- Memory Palace showed how place-based interaction can improve retention.

Stochastic Parrot combines those ideas into a single product direction:
- world-driven interaction
- concept anchoring and recall
- progression and challenge pacing
- narrative that keeps people coming back

## Product translation
What we carried forward:
1. **Interactive-first learning loops**
2. **Visible progression and replayability**
3. **Retention as a core outcome, not a side effect**

What we added:
1. stronger world-building and atmosphere
2. scene/encounter orchestration
3. production-ready UX + testing discipline
